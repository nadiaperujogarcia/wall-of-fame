Submit a kudo

Click the "Give a kudo" button on the Confluence board — it opens the Google Form in a new tab. Fill in the person's name, their value, and your message. Done. It appears on the board automatically as a mystery box.

Leadership / admin only

Reveal at all-hands

Open the Wall of Fame Google Sheet → click the Kudos tab → find the person's row → uncheck the pending box. The board updates within 30 seconds. That's the only action required from leadership.

All-hands day — step by step
1

Before the meeting — review pending kudos

Open the Wall of Fame Google Sheet → Kudos tab. Every row with the pending box ticked (✅) is a mystery box on the board. Check the name and message to prepare your reveal moment.

2

Share your screen showing the Confluence board

Open the Wall of Fame Confluence page on your screen. The mystery boxes are visible to the audience. Keep the Google Sheet open in another tab.

3

Announce the person, then reveal

Say the person's name live. Then switch to the Google Sheet tab → Kudos tab → find their row → uncheck the pending box. Switch back to the Confluence board tab — within 30 seconds the mystery box becomes their card.

Tip: have both the Confluence tab and the Google Sheet tab open side by side on a second monitor if you have one, so you can reveal without switching screens.
4

Repeat for each recipient

Uncheck one at a time so each reveal gets its own moment. The board refreshes automatically every 30 seconds — or you can manually refresh the Confluence page for an instant update.

Don't uncheck all pending boxes at once before the meeting — the whole point is revealing them live!
Quick reference cheatsheet

Google Sheet — Kudos tab

  • Pending ✅ = mystery box (hidden from team)
  • Pending ☐ = revealed card (visible to all)
  • To reveal: just uncheck the box in column H
  • To add manually: fill in a new row, tick pending ✅
  • Color 0–7 = which sticky note colour to use

Timing & refresh

  • Board refreshes every 30 seconds automatically
  • Manual refresh: reload the Confluence page
  • Form → Kudos tab: automatic (via Apps Script trigger)
  • New mystery boxes appear within 30 seconds of submission

Something not working?

  • Board not loading → check the Google Sheet is set to "Anyone with the link can view"
  • Mystery box not appearing → check the Kudos tab, pending should be ✅
  • Card not revealing → wait 30 seconds or refresh the page
  • Form not auto-copying → add the row manually to Kudos tab
